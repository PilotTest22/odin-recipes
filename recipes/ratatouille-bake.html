Ratatouille Bake

Description

A ratatouille bake is a flavorful and hearty dish showcasing a medley of summer vegetables, often baked in a casserole dish. It's a take on the traditional French Provençal vegetable stew called ratatouille

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time:45 mins
  • Total Time:1 hr
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cups peeled and diced eggplant
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chopped zucchini
  • 1 (14.5oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (8oz) package frozen cheese ravioli
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 2 ½-quart baking dish with cooking spray.

  2. Step 3

    3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add eggplant, onion, and garlic; cook and stir until vegetables begin to soften, about 8 minutes. Stir in zucchini, tomatoes, bell pepper, basil, parsley, salt, and black pepper; bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer until vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in ravioli and return to a boil. Cook, uncovered, stirring occasionally, until ravioli float to the top and the filling is hot, 3 to 5 minutes. Drain.

  4. Step 4

    5. Transfer ravioli to bottom of the prepared baking dish; spoon hot vegetable mixture over top. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.

  5. Step 5

    6. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbling and cheese melted, about 20 minutes.
